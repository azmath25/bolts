Grid Bolt Editor

Ready. Choose a domain or draw a bolt to begin.

Instructions:

  1. Set Grid: Adjust grid size (5-20) and click "Set Grid"
  2. Choose Domain: Click cells to mark the allowed region (green)
  3. Draw Bolt: Click lattice points to create a path. Points must be adjacent and turns must be 90°. Click the starting point to close the loop.
  4. Assign Sign: After drawing, assign + or - signs to vertices (alternating)
  5. Move Edge: Click an edge, then use arrow keys (←→ for vertical, ↑↓ for horizontal)
  6. Swap Rectangle: Select two diagonal points with matching sign conditions to swap positions

Rules:

  • Blue lines = bolt path, Green points = positive sign, Red points = negative sign
  • Vertical edges move left/right, horizontal edges move up/down
  • Rectangle swap: (a,b)⁻ and (c,d)⁻ → (a,d) and (c,b), or (a,d)⁺ and (c,b)⁺ → (a,b) and (c,d)
  • All movements must keep endpoints inside the domain
